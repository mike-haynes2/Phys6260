Term Project (45%)

    You will formulate, solve, and present a class project. This is an open-ended project in which you will pose a research question, devise a plan, and write a program to explore the system. You are free to choose a particular field of physics -- astrophysics, physics of living systems, condensed matter, non-linear dynamics, etc.
    Students must consult with the instructor on the viability and scope of the question before finalizing it.
    Students must finalize their research question with the instructor before Friday, February 28 (10% of grade)
    Progress report due: Friday, March 28 (15% of grade)
    Poster presentations: Monday, April 14 & Wednesday, April 16 (25% of grade)
    Final report due: Tuesday, April 29 (50% of grade)
